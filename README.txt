A simple user documentation plugin for WordPress.

== Description ==

UserDocs lets you create a section of your site dedicated to product documentation. Create anything from software documentation, to how-toâ€™s, to product specs, the possibilities are endless. Spend less time answering support requests, get UserDocs!

Besides a good feature rich product and amazing support; Product Documentation also plays an important role in terms of customer satisfaction and revenue. If there is no documentation available on the product site,\ customers will be forced to submit a support ticket costing your team valuable time.

Self help guides allow customers to go through frequently asked questions and general queries and help them resolve small issues on their own. Good documentation contains easy navigation and a clean layout with better readability. Step by step explanation with screenshots helps too. UserDocs has you covered.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `userdocs` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Place `[userdocs]` in a sidebar widget.
4. Your theme will take care of the display.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Q: I just added a UserDocs post and I get a 404 error when trying to view it. =
A: Clear your site permalinks by visiting Settings > Permalinks in the WordPress admin area.
